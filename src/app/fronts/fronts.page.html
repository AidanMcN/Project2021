FRONTS , Home Isobars Fronts Fronts Fronts explained There are three types of fronts each meaning different things. There names are a warm front , a cold front and a occlusion front. the meaning of the word front is the edge of air mass. A warm front on a wather map looks like a red line with red hills on it, it means the temperture will increse and the rain will stop or decrese. A cold front means the oppisite you can see a cold front on a weather map it looks like a blue line with blue triangles on top of it . And lastly the occlusion front brings similar weather to a warm front but but it will soon be a cold front on a weather map it looks like a purple line with both purple triangles and hills on it.
